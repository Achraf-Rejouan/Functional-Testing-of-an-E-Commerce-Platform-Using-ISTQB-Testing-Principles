## Description

During **Step 1 (Personal Information)** of the guest checkout flow, the form contains a mandatory **Terms & Conditions** checkbox with an embedded hyperlink to the full T&C document. When testers click this link to review the Terms & Conditions prior to accepting them, the link navigates to a page that is **unavailable or returns a broken/404 response within the demo frame**.

As a result:
- Users cannot review the Terms & Conditions document before accepting them
- This constitutes a **legal and compliance risk** in any production-like scenario
- The test case TC-023 (Full Guest Checkout) was marked **BLOCKED** as a result, because informed consent to T&C could not be verified

While this issue exists in the **demo environment**, its presence on a publicly accessible demo site that represents the product creates a negative impression and could cause a live deployment to carry this defect forward if not caught during QA.

---

## Steps to Reproduce

1. Navigate to `https://demo.prestashop.com/#/en/front`
2. Add any product to the cart
3. Go to the cart and click **PROCEED TO CHECKOUT**
4. On Step 1 — Personal Information — select **"Order as a guest"**
5. Fill in first name, last name, and email
6. Scroll down to locate the **"I agree to the terms and conditions"** checkbox
7. Click the hyperlinked text **"terms and conditions"** (not the checkbox — click the link itself)
8. Observe the resulting page

**Expected frequency:** Reproducible 100% of the time.

---

## Expected Result

The Terms & Conditions link opens a readable, fully formatted page (or modal) containing the legal text of the store's Terms of Service and Return Policy. The user is able to:
- Read and review the T&C document
- Close or navigate back to the checkout page with the checkbox still visible

The checkout flow is unaffected.

---

## Actual Result

The Terms & Conditions link leads to a **broken or unavailable page** within the demo iframe context. The page either:
- Returns a **404 Not Found** error, or
- Displays a blank/incomplete page with no T&C content

This leaves the user unable to review what they are agreeing to before completing the registration or guest checkout step. The test case TC-023 was marked **BLOCKED** as a consequence, as proceeding past this step without verified T&C access constitutes a test environment integrity failure.

---

## Impact

- **Legal / Compliance Impact:** Users cannot read what they are consenting to — this would be a GDPR / consumer rights violation in a production environment.
- **Functional Impact:** TC-023 (full guest checkout) and TC-025 (payment step) could not be fully executed — 2 test cases blocked.
- **Reputational Impact:** The demo site is publicly accessible; broken T&C links create a negative impression of product quality.
- **Reproducibility:** 100% reproducible.

## Suggested Fix

1. **Immediate (Demo Fix):** Create or link a placeholder Terms & Conditions CMS page within the PrestaShop demo instance at the expected URL path.

2. **Long-term (Production Fix):** Ensure the CMS page for Terms & Conditions (typically at `/en/content/3-terms-and-conditions-of-use`) is:
   - Created and published in the PrestaShop back-office under **Catalog → Pages**
   - Reachable by the URL embedded in the checkout T&C checkbox link
   - Styled consistently with the rest of the storefront
   - Opening in a new browser tab to preserve the checkout session

3. **Verification:** After fix, re-execute TC-023 and TC-025 to confirm the checkout flow completes end-to-end.
